These are the current onboarding of templates in the Assentis:
1. We receive an intake to create or update content:  https://app.smartsheet.com/b/form/4bfa0f63cf694c43ae7150b7750594d4
2. We draft it and get it approved by the business/requestor.
3. Then it goes into SharePoint for Marketing and Compliance approvals.
4. Upon approval, we build it in Assentis and include it in the next available prod upload.
5. For non-Assentis content, we coordinate with HOV and/or create Jira tickets/CIIs to complete the build.
